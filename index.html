Where This Comes From

PrimalMail draws on two main voices in the raw / primal space.

AV

Aajonus Vonderplanitz

Author of We Want to Live and founder of the Primal Diet — a raw-food framework built around raw meat, raw dairy, raw eggs, and raw fats, and a rejection of cooked and processed food. His work remains the foundational text for most of today's raw-animal-food community.

🐐

Goatis

Creator behind the YouTube channel @GoatisReviews (192K+ subscribers), who describes himself as having founded the modern raw-meat and anti-vegan movement in 2016. His videos are a major reference point for this newsletter's takes on raw-meat culture online.
