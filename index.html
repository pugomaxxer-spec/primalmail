AV

Aajonus Vonderplanitz

Wrote We Want to Live, built the Primal Diet, died in 2013. Raw meat, raw dairy, raw eggs, raw fat, basically nothing cooked. Almost everyone in the raw-animal-food space today, whether they say his name or not, is working from ground he covered first.

Goatis

Runs @GoatisReviews on YouTube, north of 192K subscribers. Says he started the modern raw-meat and anti-vegan movement back in 2016 — I can't verify that claim, but the channel's real and it's where a lot of this culture actually lives online right now.
